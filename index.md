I am an economist with a (very) broad interest in **applied microeconomics**. Yet I'm (still) able to fit my current research in at least one of three fields: **behavioral economics** (🧠), **environmental economics** (🌱), and **development economics** (🌎). My work in behavioral economics responds to  strong interest in understanding how we behave in general. My work on environmental and development economics responds to a more urgent need for specific actions.  

I live in [Uppsala](https://visitsweden.com/where-to-go/middle-sweden/uppland/uppsala/){:target="_blank"}, Sweden, where I work as a **postdoc** at [SLU](https://www.slu.se/en/departments/economics/research/research-groups/environmental-economics/){:target="_blank"}. There, I'm a member of the **environmental economics group** within the **department of economics**.

These days you can find me in **Buenos Aires, Argentina**. The reasons is that I'm doing some work there as a **visiting fellow at [JILAEE](https://www.jilaee.org/)**. If you are nearby, **please reach out!**
