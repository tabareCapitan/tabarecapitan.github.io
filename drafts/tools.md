## Zotero

1. change format of citation key (useful to have easy-to-remember stable citations in Latex)
- go to preferences>betterBibTex
- default is: auth.lower+shorttitle(3,3)+year
- change for: authEtAl+'.'+year
- more info: https://retorque.re/zotero-better-bibtex/citing/#configurable-citekey-generator
